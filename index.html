About Urbanflix

Thank you for using Urbanflix, your go-to app for discovering and organizing your favorite movies and TV shows. With Urbanflix, you can search for new movies and TV shows to watch, add them to your watchlist, and keep track of what you've watched. To get started, simply sign up and start browsing our library of popular movies and TV shows. You can search by title, genre, or even by actor or director. Once you find something you like, simply click the "Add to Watchlist" button to save it to your personal watchlist. You can access your watchlist at any time to see what you've added, mark items as watched, or remove them from your list. And with our recommendation engine, we'll suggest new titles based on your viewing history and preferences, so you'll never run out of things to watch. Thank you for choosing Urbanflix as your movie and TV show companion. We hope you enjoy using our app and discovering your next favorite show. Best regards, The Urbanflix Team !
